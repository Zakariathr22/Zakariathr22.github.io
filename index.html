About Me

My name is Zakaria Tahri, a Software Engineer who mostly wrangles .NET, WinAppSDK, and WinUI into creating desktop apps that (hopefully) don’t crash. I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science (Software Engineering & Information Systems), which basically means I spent years learning how to tell computers what to do while they still somehow manage to sass me back.

On the technical side, I can work with C#, .NET, XAML, WinUI 3, Windows App SDK, a bit of web tech, and I’m curious about AI, cloud stuff, and maybe even game dev in the future. (Because why stop at making Windows apps when you can also accidentally create Skynet?)

I live in Algeria, powered mostly by tea . When I’m not coding, you’ll probably catch me playing my favorite videogame The Longing, or watching Ali's live streams on TikTok, or running around outside trying to convince myself that I enjoy jogging.

My Work

Basically, I work on desktop applications. Here are some highlights:

  • Contributions in WinUI Gallery : Most of the time I do this just so I can proudly say I’m contributing to a Microsoft project (and feel like I’m important for at least five minutes ).
